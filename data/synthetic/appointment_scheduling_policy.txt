APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING POLICY
================================
Document Type: Patient Services Policy
Version: 3.0
Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
This document outlines the appointment scheduling policies for all departments within our healthcare system. These policies apply to all new and existing patients seeking in-person or telehealth consultations.

2. AVAILABLE DEPARTMENTS AND SPECIALTIES
------------------------------------------
The following departments are available for appointment scheduling:

Primary Care:
- General Practice / Family Medicine
- Internal Medicine
- Pediatrics
- Geriatrics

Specialty Care:
- Cardiology
- Dermatology
- Endocrinology
- Gastroenterology
- Neurology
- Oncology
- Orthopedics
- Psychiatry and Mental Health
- Pulmonology
- Rheumatology
- Urology

Women's Health:
- Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB/GYN)
- Maternal-Fetal Medicine
- Breast Health

3. HOW TO BOOK AN APPOINTMENT
--------------------------------
Option 1 - Online (Preferred):
  - Visit portal.healthsystem.org
  - Log in with your patient credentials
  - Click "Schedule New Appointment"
  - Select department, provider, date, and time
  - Confirm appointment and receive email confirmation

Option 2 - Phone:
  - Call our scheduling line: 1-800-HEALTH-1
  - Available Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM
  - Saturday, 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM
  - Have your insurance card and patient ID ready

Option 3 - In-Person:
  - Visit any of our reception desks during business hours
  - Walk-ins accepted for urgent care only

4. APPOINTMENT AVAILABILITY BY DEPARTMENT
-------------------------------------------
Cardiology:
  - New patient appointments: Available within 5-7 business days
  - Follow-up appointments: Available within 2-3 business days
  - Available slots: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  - Urgent cardiac concerns: Same-day slots reserved daily

General Practice / Family Medicine:
  - New patient appointments: Available within 1-2 business days
  - Follow-up appointments: Same-day or next-day availability
  - Available slots: Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Orthopedics:
  - New patient appointments: Available within 3-5 business days
  - Post-surgical follow-ups: Prioritized within 1-2 days
  - Available slots: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Dermatology:
  - New patient appointments: Available within 7-10 business days
  - Follow-up appointments: Available within 5 business days
  - Telehealth option available for photo consultations

Psychiatry and Mental Health:
  - Initial evaluations: Available within 7 business days
  - Therapy sessions: Weekly or bi-weekly scheduling available
  - Crisis support: Same-day slots reserved, call scheduling line

5. REQUIRED INFORMATION FOR BOOKING
--------------------------------------
All patients must provide the following at the time of booking:
- Full legal name and date of birth
- Insurance provider name and member ID
- Referring physician name (for specialist appointments, if applicable)
- Reason for visit (brief description)
- Preferred contact number
- Emergency contact name and phone number

6. NEW PATIENT REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------
New patients must complete registration at least 24 hours before their first appointment:
- Complete the new patient registration form online or at the front desk
- Upload or bring a valid government-issued photo ID
- Bring insurance card (original, not a photo)
- Bring list of current medications including dosages
- Arrive 20 minutes early on the day of appointment to complete paperwork

7. RESCHEDULING POLICY
------------------------
- Appointments may be rescheduled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time at no charge
- Rescheduling within 24 hours may result in limited availability
- Patients can reschedule online via the portal or by calling the scheduling line

8. CANCELLATION POLICY
------------------------
- Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the appointment
- Late cancellations (less than 24 hours notice): $35 fee applies
- No-show fee: $50 for primary care, $75 for specialty appointments
- Three consecutive no-shows may result in discharge from the practice
- Emergency cancellations are reviewed case-by-case

9. WALK-IN AND URGENT CARE
-----------------------------
- Urgent Care clinic accepts walk-ins daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM
- Wait times vary; average wait is 30-60 minutes
- Urgent care is for non-emergency medical issues only
- For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 or go to the Emergency Department

10. REFERRAL POLICY
--------------------
- Some specialist appointments require a referral from a primary care provider
- Referrals are typically processed within 2 business days
- Referral authorization number must be provided at the time of booking
- Expired referrals (older than 90 days) will not be accepted

11. APPOINTMENT REMINDERS
---------------------------
Patients receive automated reminders:
- 72 hours before the appointment via email
- 24 hours before the appointment via SMS (if opted in)
- 2 hours before the appointment via automated phone call
Patients can confirm, cancel, or reschedule directly from the reminder message.
